cheatsheet de Inglés A1

Gramática Básica

Presente Simple

Para rutinas y hechos generales

Estructura Afirmativa Negativa Interrogativa
I/You/We/They work don't work Do... work?
He/She/It works doesn't work Does... work?

Ej: She teaches English. / They don't eat meat.

Verbos "to be" y "to have"

Verbo Afirmativa Negativa Interrogativa
to be I am, You are, He/She/It is I'm not, You aren't, He/She/It isn't Are you...? Is he...?
to have I/You/We/They have I/You/We/They don't have Do... have?

Artículos y Adjetivos Posesivos

Artículos: a/an (singular), the (específico)

Posesivos: my, your, his, her, its, our, your, their

Vocabulario Esencial

Objetos Cotidianos

Casa Food Ropa
table, chair, bed apple, bread, milk shirt, shoes, hat

Números y Colores

Números: one, two, three... ten

Colores: red, blue, green, yellow, black, white

Rutina Diaria

  • Wake up → Despertar
  • Brush teeth → Cepillarse los dientes
  • Have breakfast → Desayunar
  • Go to bed → Acostarse

Habilidades Clave

Saludar: "Hello! My name is..."

Dar información: "I'm from Spain / I have a dog"

Seguir instrucciones: "Open the book / Write your name"

Errores Comunes

❌ "He go to school" → ✅ "He goes to school"

❌ "She is a apple" → ✅ "She is an apple"

Ejercicios Prácticos

1. Completa con "have" o "has":

She ___ a red car. → has

2. Escribe la forma correcta:

They ___ (not/eat) meat. → don't eat

3. Traduce al inglés:

¿Cuántos años tienes? → How old are you?
